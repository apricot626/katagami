How to make a gathered skirt (adult)

Sew two rectangles and run elastic through the waist — the easiest skirt there is, and a perfect first sewing project. The gather ratio sets the fabric width and how full it looks.

1. Materials & tools

Materials (adult skirt)

  • Fabric (cotton, linen or lawn) — 110 cm wide × (skirt length + fold-over + 10 cm), two pieces
  • Waist elastic (2–3 cm wide) — the waist measurement

Tools

  • Sewing machine (or needle and thread)
  • Fabric marker or pencil
  • Ruler and scissors
  • Iron
  • Pins or clips

2. Make the pattern (auto-generated in Katagami)

With the Katagami pattern tool, you just enter the finished size and it drafts a seam-allowance-included pattern, tiled to print at actual size on Letter or A4.

  1. Open the tool
    Open the pattern tool and choose “Adult clothes” → “Gathered skirt (adult)” from the tabs at the top.
  2. Enter the size
    Enter waist, skirt length and gather ratio. A higher ratio = fuller gathers.
  3. Set the seam allowance
    Use the slider at the bottom (beginners: 1.0–1.5 cm / about 3/8–5/8 in).
  4. Print
    Press “Print (actual size)”, then in the print dialog set Scale = 100% and turn “Fit to page” OFF. Check the 50 mm calibration box on the first guide sheet with a ruler.

3. Cutting the fabric

  1. Fold and cut
    Fold the fabric right sides together and place the pattern's fold edge on the fold.
  2. Cut 2
    Cut a front and a back (2 pieces).
  3. Finish the edges
    Zigzag or serge the side and hem edges.

4. Sewing

4-1. Sew the sides

  1. Sew the front and back right sides together at both sides; press open.

4-2. Make the waist casing

  1. Fold the waist under by the seam allowance, then by the elastic width plus ease; press.
  2. Stitch around, leaving a 2–3 cm elastic opening.

4-3. Thread the elastic

  1. Thread the elastic, fit to the waist, sew the ends and close the opening.

4-4. Hem

  1. Double-fold and topstitch the hem; press to finish.

5. Tips & variations

  • Fuller skirt: increase the gather ratio for more volume.
  • Soft fabric: lawn and double gauze gather beautifully.
  • Pockets: add in-seam pockets before sewing the sides.
