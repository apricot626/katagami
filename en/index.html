Katagami is a free web tool that drafts sewing patterns from the measurements you choose. Print the tiled pages — seam allowance included — at actual size on Letter or A4. Beginner-friendly sewing guides are available too (in Japanese).

Make a pattern free

From pattern to print in 3 steps

  1. Choose a pattern
    61+ patterns across clothes, accessories and pet wear.
  2. Set the size
    Pick the finished measurements, adjustable in 1 cm steps. Presets included.
  3. Print
    Auto-tiled to Letter/A4. Print at 100% scale for a true-to-size pattern.

Privacy Policy

Katagami (“the Service”) is a free, individually run sewing-pattern tool.

Handling of measurement data

The measurements you enter are processed only within your browser. They are never sent to a server or shared externally.

Setups you “Save” are stored in your browser's localStorage. This information is not transmitted externally, and saved setups can be deleted from the screen at any time. Clearing your browser data also removes them.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

The Service uses Google Analytics 4 to understand access trends. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect access information. The data collected is anonymous and does not identify individuals. If you prefer not to be tracked, please use the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

For Google's privacy practices, see the Google Privacy Policy.

Advertising / affiliates

The Service displays affiliate advertising.

As an Amazon Associate, the Service earns from qualifying purchases.

It also carries advertising links through Rakuten Affiliate and Moshimo Affiliate. If you buy a product via these links, the Service may receive a commission. This does not add any cost for you.

Cookie use associated with ad delivery follows each ad provider's policy.

Disclaimer

Pattern dimensions are approximate. Results vary with material, seam allowance and individual differences. The operator is not liable for any damages arising from use of the Service.
